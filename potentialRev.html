A Few Things Before We Start Selling


Total Cost of Lemons

Average cost of Lemons is $1.08/lemon.
Therefore the total cost of lemons is $1.08 * number of lemons


Total Cost of Sugar

Average cost of a spoonful of sugar is $.02/spoonful.
Therefore the total cost of sugar is $.02 * number of spoonfuls of sugar = $5.
